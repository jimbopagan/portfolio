About

I am a Full Stack web developer. By degree I am a mechanical engineer with background in aerospace and high voltage capacitor manufacturing. Programming was a hobby and as I grew into my mechanical engineering career I found that hobby to be something I am very passionate about more so than my major. Now after going to VSchool and more than 600 hours of coding I am transitioning to make a career out of this hobby. I am passionate about learning. I want to keep practicing in the languages I started and build on that knowledge. And I get more comfortable add to that by learning other languages. I love that most of my apps are based around my hobbies which is great to be able to work on something I am passionate about and in a field I am passionate about.

I am also the founder of Tampa Javascript meetup group. We get together every two weeks and do JS coding challenges.
